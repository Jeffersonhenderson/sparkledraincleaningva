Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do you clear clogs from yard drains?

Yes, we do. Yard drains often get clogged with leaves, soil, and debris, especially after rainfall or when the drainage slope isn’t properly maintained. Our plumbers use hydrojetting and specialized drain snakes to flush out debris without damaging your drain lines or the surrounding landscape.

2. Can I contract your team for routine drain maintenance?

Absolutely. In fact, most property owners in USA choose us for scheduled drain maintenance to prevent sudden backups and costly repairs. With our maintenance plan, our plumbers visit periodically to inspect and flush your drain lines, keeping them free-flowing all year round.

3. Do you repair broken drains?

Depending on the extent of the damage, we use trenchless methods to repair your drain lines without digging up the surface. But when excavation is needed, our team restores the area afterward, leaving it looking just as it was before.

4. How often should I inspect my building drains?

For most properties, we recommend a professional drain inspection at least once every 12 to 18 months. However, if your property handles heavy water usage like restaurants, hotels, or large commercial buildings, it’s best to schedule inspections more frequently.

5. Do you offer discounts for routine maintenance services?

Yes, we provide priority scheduling for customers who sign up for routine maintenance plans, and we charge their future drain cleaning or repair services at a discounted rates.

6. Do you offer drain cleaning for large buildings?

Yes, we do. We provide complete drain cleaning services for large buildings, including commercial properties such as hotels, offices, and serviced apartments. We also offer contractual maintenance plans to keep the entire drainage system of your building running smoothly all year round.
